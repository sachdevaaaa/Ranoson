# Ranoson Springs - Company Profile

## 1. Company Overview

**Ranoson Springs** is a prominent group of companies based in India, specializing in the manufacturing of precision springs, wire forms, and spring-making machinery. Established in 1988, the group has grown to become a key player in the industrial tools and components sector.

### Key Entities
*   **Ranoson Springs Pvt. Ltd.**
    *   **Established:** June 24, 1988
    *   **Location:** New Delhi (Headquarters), Greater Noida (Manufacturing)
    *   **Certification:** ISO 9001:2008
*   **Ranoson Products Private Limited**
    *   **Established:** Demerged in 2016
    *   **Location:** Haridwar, Uttarakhand
    *   **Certification:** ISO 9001:2015
*   **Ranoson Machines Private Limited**
    *   **Location:** Noida, Uttar Pradesh
    *   **Focus:** Manufacturing of industrial machines and tools.

## 2. Product Portfolio

Ranoson manufactures a diverse range of high-precision components for automotive, agricultural, and general industrial applications.

### Springs
*   **Compression Springs:** Designed to absorb energy and provide resistance. Available in various shapes:
    *   Helical
    *   Conical
    *   Concave (Barrel)
    *   Convex (Hourglass)
    *   *Wire Sizes:* 0.1mm to 7.00mm
*   **Tension (Extension) Springs:** Designed to resist pulling forces.
*   **Torsion Springs:** Designed to exert rotational force.
*   **Double Torsion Springs:** For specialized rotational applications.
*   **Other Types:** Ring springs, heavy compression springs, flat springs, car springs.

### Other Components
*   **Wire Forms:** Custom-designed bent wire parts for various mechanisms.
*   **Sheet Metal Solutions:** Stampings and other sheet metal products.

## 3. Manufacturing Capabilities & Tools

The company utilizes advanced infrastructure and technology to ensure high-quality production.

### Machinery Used
*   **CNC Spring Coiling Machines:** For precise and automated coiling of springs.
*   **CNC Spring Forming Machines:** For complex wire forming and spring shaping.
*   **Spring End Grinding Machines:** For finishing spring ends (e.g., grinding them flat).
*   **Heat Treatment Furnaces:** For hardening and tempering springs to achieve desired mechanical properties.

### Quality Assurance & Testing
*   **Load Testing Machines:** To verify the load-bearing capacity of springs.
*   **Torque Testers:** For measuring rotational force in torsion springs.
*   **Life Cycle Testers:** To determine the fatigue life of components.
*   **Labs:** In-house facilities for electrical continuity testing, with access to N.A.B.L accredited labs for chemical and mechanical analysis.

## 4. Machinery Manufactured

Through its subsidiary, **Ranoson Machines Private Limited**, the group also manufactures and exports machinery used in the spring industry.

### Machine Offerings
*   **CNC Compression Spring Coiling Machines**
*   **Wire Forming Machines**
*   **CNC Camless MCB Aircoil Winding Machines**
*   **Torsion Spring Making Machines**
*   **Terminal Coiler Machines**
*   **Spring End Grinding Machines** (e.g., Model RSG-80 DH)

### Industrial Tools
They also produce critical tooling for these machines, often made from high-grade Carbide or Steel:
*   Wire Guides
*   Feed Rollers
*   Wire Coiling Fingers
